About Me

During my high school education with Lakewood High School, graduated in 2015, I had discovered a passion for coding and computer science. I took as many courses as possible; from web development to AP computer science to an independent study with a small group to build our school's TigerBook (a Facebook type clone). After working with the front end and back end during that time, I had intended to pursue the career further. Life got in the way for some time, but I am currently in training to become a full stack developer through the University of Denver.

The University of Denver was able to provide a recap of what I had known initially regarding web development, and expanded even further. Through the University I have been able to learn new languages, the collaboration that is required for projects, and how to troubleshoot errors and find solutions to your challenges.

I am very proud of the work that I have been able to produce, please peruse the portfolio and feel free to reach out regarding any questions or opportunities.

On a personal note - I spend my free time with my husband and my two cats. If we're home, we're likely watching movies or playing video games. If we're not home, you can find us getting food or spending time on the lake paddle boarding. If we're not in Colorado, you can find us in California, likely in Disneyland.
